Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 17, 2025

Guess the Number is a simple browser game. We do not collect or store any personal information.

How the Game Works

At Level 1, you guess a number between 1 and 100 with 5 attempts. Each new level increases the range by 100 and adds 2 extra attempts, up to Level 100.

What Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Your progress is saved only in your current browser session and is cleared when you close the tab.
